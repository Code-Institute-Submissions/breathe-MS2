Breathing Techniques & Benefits

Box Breathing

  • Also known as square breathing.
  • Tempo: 4:4:4:4
  • Benefits may include: heightened performance and concentration and a powerful stress-reliever
  • Ideally repeat the breathing cycle four times in one sitting

Relaxing Breathing

  • Also known as pranayama.
  • Tempo: 4:7:8:0
  • Benefits include: stress reduction, relaxation and aids sleep
  • Ideally practise this breathing technique twice a day

Calming Breathing

  • Tempo: 7:0:11:0
  • Benefits include: quick relaxation, reduces anxiety, gives a general feeling of calm
  • Recommended to practise this for 5-10 minutes at a time
