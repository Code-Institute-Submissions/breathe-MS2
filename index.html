Breathing Techniques

Box Breathing

An alternate name is square breathing. The tempo is an easy 4:4:4:4 i.e. inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds and so on. Benefits can include heightened performance and concentration. Box breathing should be repeated four times in one sitting.

Relaxing Breathing

This breathing type is also known as pranayama. The tempo is 4:7:8:0. When practised regularly, benefits may include stress reduction, relaxation and helps with any sleep issues. This should be practised twice a day for best results.

Calming Breathing

The tempo for this breathing exercise is 7:0:11:0. The benefits for this include quick relaxation, reduction in anxiety, and it can result in a general feeling of calm. It is recommended to practise this for 5-10 minutes at a time.
